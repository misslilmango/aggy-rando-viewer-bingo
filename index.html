How to Play & Board Options

  • Bingo Etiquette: Please keep it fun and fair! Do not use your card as an excuse to backseat, influence gameplay, or fish for squares in chat. Let outcomes happen naturally!
  • Randomizer Modes: These pools contain items tailored specifically to tracking LilAggy's weekly randomizer gameplay.
  • Elden Ring Bingo Mode: This pool is universal and built to work for ANY streamer matchup, including Bingo Brawlers events. When playing, generic text indicators like "Gets first square" apply to the streamer you are currently rooting for. Explicit targets like "Opponent gets first square" track their match rival.
  • Boards Reset at 6AM EST/EDT Daily. If you need multiple cards before the reset, simply append a number to your username (e.g., username1).
  • Make Suggestions: Please provide your input on the Discord server by tagging me @lil.mango!
